My Favorite Video Games!

The Ascent is a cyberpunk-themed action role-playing video game

  • Isometric view overlooking the characters
  • Couch co-op
  • Customizable characters
Rating ★★★★★

Halo Reach is a first-person shooter video game.

  • Takes place in the year 2552
  • Couch co-op
  • Follows Nobel Team (A group of Spartans)
Rating ★★★★★

Dysmantle is an post-apocalyptic open world RPG where you ruin everything!

  • Fight zombie-like monsters
  • Couch co-op
  • Literally everything can eventually be destroyed
Rating ★★★★★

Alien Isolation is a horror survival game.

  • Play as Ellen Ripley's Daughter Amanda
  • Single player
  • Try to sneak around a space station where humans, aliens, and AI's want to kill you
Rating ★★★★★

For The King is a rogue-like table top adventure.

  • Battle against enemies on a constantly changing board
  • Couch co-op
  • Turn based combat
Rating ★★★★★
